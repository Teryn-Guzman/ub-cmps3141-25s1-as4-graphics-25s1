Other Design Principles

Alignment was applied to create a clean, structured layout that improves readability. Contrast was also used through color and typography to highlight important information, while proximity helped group related elements, making each design easier to understand.

Design Reflection

Applying these design principles enhanced the overall clarity and cohesion of the business identity. Repetition strengthened the brand recognition, while alignment, contrast, and proximity contributed to a more polished and effective visual communication system.
